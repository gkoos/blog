I launched a new newsletter: Import Chaos.

This newsletter is a separate space for concise engineering notes, experiments, and in-progress ideas from real projects.

What it covers

  • Go internals and runtime behavior
  • JavaScript and TypeScript networking and resilience
  • Modern database topics I am researching for The Database Safari
  • Production war stories, tradeoffs, and postmortem-style lessons
  • My fail of the week (I do make mistakes)
  • Build notes from open source work such as fetch-kit and confluence2md

The core theme stays the same as this blog: test assumptions, break abstractions, and learn from controlled chaos.

Frequency and format

Expect low volume: roughly one issue every 1-4 weeks, when there is something useful to share.

Most issues will be one of these:

  • Deep technical breakdowns
  • Behind-the-scenes development notes
  • Early research snippets and ideas in progress
  • Short "Your X Is Lying to You" style takes
